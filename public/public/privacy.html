Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters

We are parents building a tool for parents. We understand that health information — especially a child's — is deeply personal. Here is exactly what we collect, what we don't, and why.

The short version

✓We do not store your CGM data or screenshots
✓We do not require sign-up or collect personal information to use the app
✓Screenshots sent for AI analysis go directly to Anthropic's API and are not stored by us
✓Pattern history is stored in your browser session only — it disappears when you close the tab
✓We do not sell data. We do not serve ads. We do not use tracking pixels.
📸

CGM screenshots

When you upload a CGM screenshot for analysis, the image is converted to a base64 string in your browser and sent directly to the Anthropic API (Claude). It is not routed through our servers, and we do not store, log, or retain the image in any way.

Anthropic's handling of API data is governed by Anthropic's privacy policy. API inputs are not used to train Anthropic's models by default.

🗂️

Pattern history

The "My Pattern Profile" feature stores analysis history in your browser's memory for the current session only. This data is never sent to a server and is automatically cleared when you close or refresh the tab.

We do not use localStorage, cookies, or any persistent browser storage for health data.

📧

Email (optional)

If you choose to enter your email for the "First 90 Days guide", your email is used only to send that guide. We do not share it with third parties. You can unsubscribe at any time.

This feature is optional — the app works fully without providing an email address.

📊

Analytics

We may use basic, privacy-preserving analytics (such as page view counts) to understand how the app is being used. These tools do not collect personal information, do not use cookies, and do not track individuals across sites.

🍪

Cookies

This app does not use advertising cookies, tracking cookies, or third-party cookies. The only cookies that may be set are those strictly necessary for the app to function (such as session management, if applicable).

👶

Children's privacy

We understand this app is frequently used in the context of children with Type 1 Diabetes. We are particularly careful with this in mind. We do not collect any information that could identify a child. CGM screenshots and pattern data stay in your browser session and are not transmitted to or stored by us.

📬

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or how your data is handled, please reach out. We are committed to transparency and will respond promptly.
