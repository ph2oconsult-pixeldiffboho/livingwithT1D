Why this project exists

"We didn't just survive the diagnosis — it became part of what shaped our family's resilience and purpose."

When our daughter was diagnosed with Type 1 Diabetes, we entered a world we knew nothing about. Glucose readings that made no sense. Spikes after meals we thought were safe. Overnight rises that frightened us. The endless questions: Why did that happen? Is this normal?

Over time we learned — that the spike three hours after pizza was the fat slowing digestion, that sport sometimes causes a high before a low, that 3am rises have a name (the dawn phenomenon). And something remarkable happened: instead of defining her, diabetes became part of what shaped her resilience and purpose.

Today our daughter works for Breakthrough T1D, helping advance the search for better treatments and ultimately a cure. This app grew out of everything our family had to learn the hard way.
