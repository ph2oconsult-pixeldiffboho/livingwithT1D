Medical Disclaimer

Disclaimer & safety information

Please read this before using any part of the Living Brilliantly with T1D app.

🚨

In a medical emergency

Call 000 immediately (Australia)

If your child is unconscious, having a seizure, cannot be woken, or you believe they are in danger — do not use this app. Call 000 immediately or go to your nearest emergency department.

Signs of severe hypoglycaemia (dangerously low glucose) or DKA (diabetic ketoacidosis) require emergency medical attention.

⚕️

This app is educational only

Living Brilliantly with T1D is a learning and educational tool. It is designed to help families understand common glucose patterns and the possible reasons behind them.

This app does not provide: medical advice, insulin dosing recommendations, correction factor guidance, treatment instructions, or any individualised health guidance.

Every person with Type 1 Diabetes has a unique physiology. What applies generally may not apply to your child. Always follow the specific guidance provided by your endocrinologist, diabetes educator, and care team.

🤖

AI-generated explanations

Some features use AI (Claude by Anthropic) to generate plain-language explanations of glucose patterns. These explanations are educational in nature and are designed to help families understand what may be happening — not to guide treatment decisions.

AI explanations use "possible reasons" language deliberately. They reflect general educational patterns, not your child's specific medical situation. They should never be used to change insulin doses, timing, or any other aspect of diabetes management.

The AI is instructed to avoid dosing advice and to recommend consulting your care team for any treatment decisions.

👩‍⚕️

Always follow your care team

Your endocrinologist, diabetes educator, and care team know your child's individual needs, history, and circumstances. This app does not.

If anything in this app contradicts advice from your care team — follow your care team. If you are uncertain about any glucose reading or behaviour, contact your care team directly.

📋

Accuracy and limitations

While we aim for accuracy in all educational content, diabetes management is a complex, evolving field. Content in this app may not reflect the most recent clinical guidelines. Always verify important information with your care team.

Pattern detection is based on common educational models and may not reflect every individual's experience. Glucose behaviour varies significantly between individuals.

📬

Questions or concerns

If you have concerns about any content in this app, or believe something is incorrect or potentially harmful, please contact us so we can review and update it promptly.
